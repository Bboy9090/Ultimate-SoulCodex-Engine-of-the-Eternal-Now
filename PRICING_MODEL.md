## Overview

## Upgrade Flows

### In-App Upgrade Triggers

**Gentle Nudges (Non-Intrusive):**
1. **Compatibility Limit Reached:**
   - User tries to generate 4th compatibility analysis this month
   - Modal: "You've used all 3 free compatibility analyses this month. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited analyses and advanced insights."
   - CTAs: "Upgrade Now" | "Remind Me Later"

2. **Saved People Limit:**
   - User tries to add 6th person
   - Toast: "Free tier allows up to 5 saved people. Upgrade to Premium to save unlimited people."
   - CTA: "Learn More"

3. **AI Chat Limit:**
   - User sends 11th message this month
   - Chat message from AI: "You've reached your 10 free messages this month. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited AI guidance. [Upgrade]"

4. **Premium Feature Access:**
   - User taps "Transits Calendar" or "Progressions"
   - Modal with preview: "This is a Premium feature. Upgrade to unlock advanced insights like transits calendars, progressions, and return charts."
   - CTAs: "Start 7-Day Free Trial" | "See All Premium Features"

**Value-Focused Prompts:**
- Not framed as "you can't do this"
- Instead: "Unlock more with Premium" or "Get the full experience"
- Emphasize benefits, not restrictions

### Cancellation Flow

**Access Point:**
- Settings â†’ "Manage Subscription" â†’ Opens Stripe Customer Portal

**Stripe Portal Handles:**
- Cancel subscription (immediate or end of period)
- Update payment method
- View billing history
- Download invoices

**Cancellation Retention:**
- Before cancel: "We're sorry to see you go. Can you tell us why?" (optional feedback form)
- Offer: "Pause your subscription for 30 days instead?" (future feature)

**Post-Cancellation:**
- Premium features remain active until end of billing period
- Then downgrade to Free tier:
  - Compatibility analyses: Last 3 analyses visible, can't generate new ones beyond 3/month
  - Saved people: All people remain saved, but can't add more if >5
  - Journal entries: All entries remain visible, but can't add new ones
  - AI chat: Limited to 10 messages/month
  - PDF exports: Limited to 1/month
- Re-upgrade anytime (no data loss)

## Pricing Psychology

### Why This Pricing Works

**$9.99/Month:**
- Below $10 psychological threshold (feels affordable)
- Comparable to Netflix, Spotify, meditation apps ($8-$15/mo)
- Less than one coffee shop visit per week
- Positions as "premium but accessible"

**$89.99/Year:**
- 25% savings = strong incentive for commitment
- $7.49/month effective rate (even more affordable)
- Upfront payment improves cash flow
- Reduces churn (annual users less likely to cancel)

**7-Day Free Trial:**
- Low barrier to entry (just credit card)
- Enough time to experience full value (7 days > 3 days)
- Clear notifications prevent surprise charges
- Builds trust and reduces purchase anxiety

### Value Anchoring

**Free Tier is Generous:**
- Users can fully experience the core product
- Build trust before asking for payment
- Word-of-mouth marketing from satisfied free users

**Premium is Clear Upgrade:**
- 10x value for those who use app frequently
- Unlimited = no mental friction ("should I check compatibility again?")
- Advanced features justify the price (transits calendar, progressions worth $5-10 alone)
