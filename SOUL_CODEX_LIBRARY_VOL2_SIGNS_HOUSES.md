## Section 1 — How Signs & Houses Work Together

Planets describe **what** is acting. Signs describe **how** it acts. Houses describe **where** life expresses it. Signs and houses never change who you are; they shape how inner functions move through life.

---

## Section 2 — The Twelve Signs (Style of Expression)

Signs are styles, not personalities. They describe how energy moves, not what it wants.

### Aries — Initiation & Courage
• **Core style:** direct, immediate, instinctive.
• **Why it exists:** life needs initiation; someone must go first.
• **How it shows up:** fast responses, courage under pressure, preference for action over planning.
• **Stress pattern:** impatience, reactive behavior, burnout from constant motion.
• **Integration:** pausing strengthens courage.

### Taurus — Stability & Sustaining
• **Core style:** grounded, steady, embodied.
• **Why it exists:** life needs continuity and nourishment.
• **How it shows up:** loyalty, physical awareness, resistance to unnecessary change.
• **Stress pattern:** stubbornness, over-attachment, inertia.
• **Integration:** stability thrives when flexibility is allowed.

### Gemini — Curiosity & Adaptation
• **Core style:** mental, communicative, flexible.
• **Why it exists:** life evolves through information exchange.
• **How it shows up:** learning quickly, verbal expression, multitasking.
• **Stress pattern:** scattered focus, nervous energy, surface-level engagement.
• **Integration:** depth gives meaning to curiosity.

### Cancer — Care & Emotional Intelligence
• **Core style:** protective, sensitive, responsive.
• **Why it exists:** life survives through care and bonding.
• **How it shows up:** emotional attunement, protective instincts, memory-driven responses.
• **Stress pattern:** defensiveness, emotional withdrawal, over-protectiveness.
• **Integration:** emotional safety grows through boundaries.

### Leo — Expression & Vitality
• **Core style:** creative, expressive, heart-centered.
• **Why it exists:** life needs authentic self-expression.
• **How it shows up:** leadership through presence, creativity, desire to be seen.
• **Stress pattern:** ego attachment, validation-seeking, performance pressure.
• **Integration:** expression becomes powerful when it serves meaning.

### Virgo — Refinement & Service
• **Core style:** analytical, precise, improvement-oriented.
• **Why it exists:** life evolves through refinement.
• **How it shows up:** attention to detail, problem-solving, helpfulness.
• **Stress pattern:** over-criticism, perfectionism, self-doubt.
• **Integration:** service includes compassion for self.

### Libra — Balance & Relationship
• **Core style:** harmonizing, relational, fair-minded.
• **Why it exists:** life needs cooperation and balance.
• **How it shows up:** diplomacy, aesthetic awareness, conflict mediation.
• **Stress pattern:** indecision, people-pleasing, avoidance of conflict.
• **Integration:** harmony deepens when truth is included.

### Scorpio — Depth & Transformation
• **Core style:** intense, probing, emotionally courageous.
• **Why it exists:** life evolves through transformation.
• **How it shows up:** emotional depth, psychological insight, resilience through crisis.
• **Stress pattern:** control issues, secrecy, emotional extremes.
• **Integration:** power stabilizes through conscious release.

### Sagittarius — Expansion & Truth
• **Core style:** exploratory, philosophical, future-oriented.
• **Why it exists:** life grows through perspective.
• **How it shows up:** optimism, belief systems, desire for freedom.
• **Stress pattern:** restlessness, avoidance of responsibility, excess.
• **Integration:** truth gains power when grounded.

### Capricorn — Structure & Mastery
• **Core style:** disciplined, strategic, goal-oriented.
• **Why it exists:** life matures through responsibility.
• **How it shows up:** long-term planning, endurance, leadership through example.
• **Stress pattern:** rigidity, emotional suppression, workaholism.
• **Integration:** structure supports life — it doesn’t replace it.

### Aquarius — Innovation & Individuality
• **Core style:** original, unconventional, future-focused.
• **Why it exists:** life evolves by breaking outdated systems.
• **How it shows up:** innovation, detachment from norms, humanitarian ideals.
• **Stress pattern:** emotional detachment, rebellion without direction, isolation.
• **Integration:** individuality strengthens when connected.

### Pisces — Empathy & Transcendence
• **Core style:** sensitive, intuitive, symbolic.
• **Why it exists:** life connects through shared feeling.
• **How it shows up:** empathy, imagination, spiritual sensitivity.
• **Stress pattern:** escapism, boundary confusion, overwhelm.
• **Integration:** compassion deepens when anchored in reality.

## Section 3 — The Twelve Houses (Life Arenas)

Houses describe where life teaches you.

### House I — Identity & Self-Expression
• **Life focus:** personal identity, first impressions, self-direction.
• **Growth:** self-awareness without self-obsession.

### House II — Values & Resources
• **Life focus:** money, values, personal resources.
• **Growth:** worth is internal before external.

### House III — Communication & Learning
• **Life focus:** communication, siblings, information exchange.
• **Growth:** meaning beyond data.

### House IV — Home & Emotional Foundation
• **Life focus:** family, roots, inner safety.
• **Growth:** belonging without stagnation.

### House V — Creativity & Joy
• **Life focus:** creativity, romance, play.
• **Growth:** joy without performance pressure.

### House VI — Work & Well-Being
• **Life focus:** daily routines, health, service.
• **Growth:** balance between effort and care.

### House VII — Relationships & Mirrors
• **Life focus:** partnerships, cooperation, reflection.
• **Growth:** self-awareness through relationship.

### House VIII — Transformation & Shared Power
• **Life focus:** intimacy, shared resources, psychological growth.
• **Growth:** trust without control.

### House IX — Meaning & Expansion
• **Life focus:** truth, travel, education.
• **Growth:** wisdom over dogma.

### House X — Purpose & Contribution
• **Life focus:** career, reputation, authority.
• **Growth:** achievement aligned with values.

### House XI — Community & Future
• **Life focus:** friendships, social causes, future goals.
• **Growth:** belonging without losing individuality.

### House XII — Subconscious & Release
• **Life focus:** inner life, endings, transcendence.
• **Growth:** letting go without escape.

## Section 4 — Combining Planet + Sign + House

Soul Codex synthesizes each placement by pairing the planet’s function with the sign’s style and the house’s arena. Example: **Moon in Sagittarius, 9th House** — emotional regulation seeks meaning and exploration; safety comes from understanding the bigger picture and trusting life’s direction. Outputs are always readable, grounded, and psychologically accurate.
